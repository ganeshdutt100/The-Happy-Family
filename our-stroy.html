Our Story

Here are some of our stories, like the places we visited, how much we enjoyed after going there, how our family felt after the trip, what we ate there, what new ideas we learned, and how the overall experience was.
🛕Prem Mandir

I went to the Prem Mandir with my family.

My family and I recently visited the beautiful Prem Mandir, enjoying quality time together amidst the serene and sacred atmosphere. A truly special trip!

Stepping into the temple, we were awestruck by its magnificent architecture and intricate carvings, creating a deeply spiritual and peaceful atmosphere. A truly enriching experience for our souls.

The post-visit gathering as a family warmed our hearts as we shared how the experience had uniquely touched each one of us.

🛕Banke Bihari ji

I went to the Banke Bihari ji with my family.

Banke Bihari Ji is a beloved form of Lord Krishna, worshipped with great devotion in the Banke Bihari temple in Vrindavan, India.

The sight of Banke Bihari ji captivates the hearts of devotees with love and devotion at the famous temple in Vrindavan, India.

Banke Bihari ji's prasad, a sacred offering from the deity, is received with devotion and gratitude by the devotees.(PEDA🍪)
Taj Mehal

My family and I visited the Taj Mahal.

Taj Mahal is a world-famous monument in Agra, India, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal.

The Taj Mahal to witness its exquisite beauty, marvel at its architectural grandeur, and experience the timeless love story behind its creation.

We had a lot of fun there and also brought back some Petha from there.

Khatu Shyam Baba

The Khatu Shyam Baba.

Khatu Shyam, also known as Shyam Baba, is a revered deity in Hinduism, particularly in the northern regions of India.

The main temple dedicated to Khatu Shyam is located in Khatu town in the Sikar district of the Indian state of Rajasthan.

He is believed to be an incarnation of Lord Krishna and is worshipped as the God of Compassion and Miracles.
